Derivatives of Functions
========================
These functions generate the first and second derivatives of the eight key functions: linear, quadratic, cubic, hyperbolic, exponential, logarithmic, logistic, and sinusoidal. They provide both the coefficients of the derivatives as well as the evaluations of them.
